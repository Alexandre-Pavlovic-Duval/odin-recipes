Cheese Pizza

Description

This is a pizza for 2 persons.

This is especially a good pizza because it's a cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Various cheeses
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Take the dough and put flour on it
  2. Stretch the dough - don't toss
  3. round the ends of the pizza dought
  4. Start puting the tomato sauce and cheese on the dough
  5. Put it on the oven to bake it
